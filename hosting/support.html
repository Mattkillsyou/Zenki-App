Frequently asked questions

I can't sign in

  • Make sure you're using your registered email and password
  • Passwords are case-sensitive
  • If you've forgotten your password, tap Forgot password? on the sign-in screen and we'll email a reset link

The app is asking for an invite code

Zenki Dojo is a private app for members of the dojo. Ask your sensei or an existing member for the current invite code. If you're a new member, email us at the address above and we'll send you one.

Pushing the "Book" button doesn't charge me

That's intentional. Zenki Dojo v1.0 doesn't process payments in-app — bookings are inquiries, and our team follows up by text or email to confirm. Payment is handled in person at the dojo.

I want to connect a heart-rate monitor

  • Heart-rate monitors must support the standard Bluetooth Heart Rate Service
  • Go to the HR Session tile on the home screen, tap Scan
  • Works with Polar H10, Wahoo Tickr, Garmin HRM-Dual/Pro, and most recent chest straps
  • Web preview doesn't support Bluetooth — use the iOS app on device

How do I turn on dark mode / change the theme?

Settings → Appearance. There are 8 themes: System, Clean Light, Clean Dark, Nostromo, Jurassic Park, Ghost in the Shell, Blade Runner, The Matrix, Sheikah Slate.

How do I change my home screen layout?

Tap Edit next to "Welcome back, " on the home screen. Drag sections to rearrange. Tap the red − to hide a section; tap the green + to restore it. Tap DONE to save.

How do I export my data?

Settings → Data → Export All Data. Produces a JSON file with everything tied to your account.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account. Confirms once, then permanently removes: - Your profile and all workouts - Your posts, messages, and follows - Your photos, DEXA scans, and bloodwork uploads - Your Firebase account

This cannot be undone.

I see something inappropriate on the feed

Tap the ••• menu in the top-right of the post, choose Report post, and pick a reason. Our team reviews reports and takes action. You can also Block user from the same menu to immediately hide all their content from your feed and messages.

Someone is messaging me I don't want to hear from

Open the conversation, tap ••• in the header → Block user. They'll vanish from your feed and messages. Alternatively, from their profile tap ••• → Block user. To undo: Settings → Privacy & Safety → Blocked Users → Unblock.

I uploaded a bloodwork or DEXA scan — who sees it?

Only you. Admins cannot view your health uploads without explicit consent. Images are processed by Anthropic Claude AI to extract biomarker values; the image itself is not retained by Anthropic.

See Privacy Policy for full details on data handling.

The app keeps signing me out

That's a bug we've fixed in v1.0. Make sure you're on the latest version — App Store → Zenki Dojo → Update.

I'm an admin and I need to manage members / products / schedule

Admin features are in the Admin tab (visible only when isAdmin: true). Need admin access? Email us.

Known issues

  • Web preview does not support Bluetooth heart-rate monitors
  • GPS route map displays only on web; on iOS you'll see distance and duration without a route visualization (map view coming in v1.0.1)
  • Google Calendar sync requires the "Link Calendar" button in the Book screen — may take a few seconds on first connect

Troubleshooting — collect info before emailing

When you email us, include: - iPhone model (Settings → General → About → Model Name) - iOS version (Settings → General → About → iOS Version) - Zenki Dojo version (Settings → scroll to bottom) - A description of what you were doing when the issue happened - Screenshots if applicable

That cuts support turnaround in half.
